Medical Facilities Available

OPD:

In OPD, clinical consultation is provided to patients and in required cases lab tests are advised. The Institute has visiting doctors providing free consultation to the Students, faculty, Non faculty Staff members and members of their family

Pharmacy

Routine medicines are available for faculty, non-faculty staff members, their dependents and students. Medicines are dispensed on the prescription of Medical Officer, Health Centre.

Click here to check the basic medicines: Medicine Available

ECG

An ECG facility is available in the health center. Employees and their dependents can avail the facility of E.C.G.

Observer Room

An observer room having semi fowler beds is available for the patients of critical care

Ambulance

Ambulance is Available 24x7 in case of any emergengy.
